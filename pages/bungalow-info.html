Tips on the Unit

Bungalow Information

A few things to know about our little house to make your stay confortable.

Electronic Locks
Doors and Locks
To OPEN -> Enter the code and turn the deadbolt away from the doorframe. To LOCK -> Hit the SCHLAGE button first, then turn the deadbolt into lock position (towards the doorframe). When opening or closing, you may need to pull the doorhandle towards you slightly to make sure the deadbolt slides in/out smoothly.
Google Nest Thermostat
Thermostat Heat/Cool
Google Nest does not have a touch-screen (tapping the face does nothing). Tap the right hand side of the unit to wake and then scroll up to increase temp and down to lower it.
TVs
Roku Streaming TV
Hit Power on the remote to go to the Roku menu screen. 'Bungalow Guest' on Netflix, HBOMax, and Paramount are available to you. If the screensaver turns on, you can power off by holding down the Power button for 2+ seconds.
Fireplace
Electric Fireplace
The Power button is in a small opening on the right side. At the bottom of the unit (on the right) there are pushbutton controls for lighting and fan (these are a little hard to see).
Sink Garbage Disposal
Kitchen/Sink
The button on the upper left corner of the sink is the garbage disposal. Run the water first and be careful with your hands! Only normal food items please!
Fire Extinguisher
Kitchen/Sink
There is a fire extinguisher in the cupboard under the kitchen sink.
First Aid Kit
Laundry Room
There is a First Aid Kit in the Laundry Room.
