Tips on the Unit

Bungalow Information

A few things to know about our little house to make your stay comfortable.

Electronic Locks
Doors and Locks
This is a deadbolt system where you still turn the lock mechanism yourself. TO OPEN: Enter the code and turn the deadbolt away from the doorframe (you may need to lightly pull the door towards you to make it slide smoothly). TO LOCK: Hit the SCHLAGE button first (blinks), then turn the deadbolt into lock position (towards the doorframe). Again, you may need to pull the doorhandle towards you slightly to make sure the deadbolt slides smoothly into the lock.
Google Nest Thermostat
Thermostat Heat/Cool
Google Nest does not have a touch-screen (tapping the face does nothing). Tap the right hand side of the unit to wake and then scroll up to increase temp and down to lower it.
TVs
Roku Streaming TV
Hit Power on the remote to go to the Roku menu screen. The TV is in Guest Mode, which allows you to set your own checkout date and then log in to your own streaming services. On the checkout date, the TV will automatically erase all your logins. If the screensaver turns on, you can power off by holding down the Power button for 2+ seconds.
Bluetooth Speaker (on mantel)
Bluetooth
Hold down the Power button (left side of buttons) for a couple of seconds to power on and prepare to sync. Find the unit name in your machine's Bluetooth settings.
Fireplace
Electric Fireplace
The Power button is in a small opening on the right side. At the bottom of the unit (on the right) there are pushbutton controls for lighting and fan (these are a little hard to see).
Sink Garbage Disposal
Kitchen/Sink
The button on the upper left corner of the sink is the garbage disposal. Run the water first and be careful with your hands! Only normal food items please!
Fire Extinguisher
Kitchen/Sink
There is a fire extinguisher in the cupboard under the kitchen sink.
First Aid Kit
Laundry Room
There is a First Aid Kit in the Laundry Room.
Neighbor Info: Art Studio
Neighbors
There is an Art Studio with a tenant in the small building next door behind the fence. They will enter and exit through the gate, but they will not park in the parking spot. It is yours.
Rules: No Parties
Neighbors
No parties are allowed on the property. We need to keep good relations with our neighbors.
